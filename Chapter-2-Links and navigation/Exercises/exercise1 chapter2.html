Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a sci-fi horror series set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. When a young boy vanishes, a small group of kids and adults uncover a mysterious government experiment, a strange girl with powers, and a portal to a dangerous alternate dimension called the Upside Down.

Key Information

  • Genre: Sci-fi, Horror, Mystery
  • Created by: The Duffer Brothers
  • Network: Netflix
  • First aired: July 15, 2016
  • Seasons: 4 (5th upcoming)

Main Cast

  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Caleb McLaughlin as Lucas Sinclair

Popular Characters

  • Eleven - A girl with telekinetic powers
  • Mike Wheeler - Brave and loyal friend
  • Jim Hopper - The town’s police chief
  • Demogorgon - A creature from the Upside Down
